RSS reader, Feedly which has recently launched a New Cloud Platform, ahead of Google Reader shut down has now introduced a premium subscription option to its content aggregation service.The development was first reported by Engadget.Feedly PRO will be available at a cost of USD 5 per month or USD 45 for a year-long subscription.Also, as a launch offer it is providing lifetime edition for USD 99 for the first 5,000 subscribers.   Few of its features will include: 1) Article search: Search within your feedly feeds.
