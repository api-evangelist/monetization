Gigaom Newsletters Dont miss out.Sign up to get all the news you need delivered right to your inbox.Suggesting that educators and students shouldn’t have to worry about the technology that helps them teach and learn, Google introduced Drive for Education on Tuesday.In the coming weeks, all Google Apps for Education users will gain unlimited data storage on Google Drive, although single files are limited to 5 terabytes in size.Also included with Drive for Education is free access to the Google Apps Vault, used for email compliance, and enhanced auditing for file activity.
