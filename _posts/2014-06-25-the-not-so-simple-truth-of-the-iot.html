The Internet of Things is exciting.By connecting machines, objects, agents (aka “things”) via the internet so that systems work more effectively, people will have the information they need to make better decisions and improve their quality of life.Sign me up.But with such a big promise one thing is clear: the IoT will be anything but simple.The IoT will be more complicated and chaotic than we expect.
