In a series of articles on TechCrunch and Digiday, Dan Greenberg of Sharethrough recently profiled a trend that’s going mainstream among apps: native monetization.Greenberg praised Facebook, Twitter, and other apps for shifting away from “traditional interruptive ad models” and integrating paid content as part of the stream that users see.With a shared vision for a future where brands create and distribute content that creates value, not ads that interrupt and annoy, Silicon Valley’s creative minds are setting the tone for the next stage of worldwide digital advertising.Other well-known apps that have pivoted to native monetization are Tumblr, Spotify, BuzzFeed, WordPress, and StumbleUpon.A Proven Model for Apps Great and Small But the promise of native monetization isn’t enticing only large platforms: ecosystem apps and independent players are also monetizing natively.
